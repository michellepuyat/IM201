Site Purpose

The purpose of the eyeskyweather.com is to provide weather updates of the tri-cities in Idaho, USA. It will provide visitors information on the current weather status. It will include the temperature, the cloud coverage, rain or snow, air pressure and humidity. This will give information to the visitors if it is good to visit the cities featured in the site.

The site is not only for tourism purpose. It aims to provide valuable information to farmers. It will provide science- based information to improve the cropping system, protocols, and long-term strategies geared towards maximizing crop yield and minimizing adverse environmental and climate impacts. It will help to improve the agricultural industry for these tri-cities.
