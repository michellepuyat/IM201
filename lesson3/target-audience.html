Target Audience

The target audience of the EyeSkyWeather.com website is described below:

  • Who: Farmers, residents living in the area, tourists who plan to visit the area
  • Age: 12 to 70
  • Income: Income levels will typically be between $20,000 and above annually.
  • Occupation: It varies from professionals, students, farmers, businessmen, and even work from home moms who are planning to visit the area
  • Device: The site visitor will most likely use their mobile phones to check for the weather update. However, businessmen and farmers will use their laptop or desktop to get more information on how to improve their crops
